Preheat your oven to 180°C fan bake. Pat the chicken dry with a paper towel, then place it in a large bowl and toss with a generous drizzle of olive oil, salt, and pepper. Arrange the chicken on an oiled wire rack over a baking tray and bake for 25-30 minutes, or until golden brown and cooked through. Meanwhile, bring a small saucepan to medium heat and add the cranberry jelly, Dijon mustard, orange juice and zest, ginger, garlic, oregano, and soy sauce. Mix to combine, then bring to a simmer and cook for 5 minutes or until smooth and heated through, stirring frequently. Transfer the cooked chicken to a large bowl and carefully pour the hot cranberry sauce over it. Stir to coat, then place the chicken back on the wire rack and bake for an additional 5-10 minutes. Serve while hot and enjoy!

  4. 2 oranges

  6. 2 garlic cloves, minced
  7. 2 Tbsp dried oregano
  8. 2 Tbsp soy sauce
